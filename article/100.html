"Very well, just one kilometer left, and we'll be safe," Xue Museng exhaled deeply.

It was wise to let Mo Fan lead the team; this guy had more experience than he, the teacher. Using the demon powder for deduction, employing feints, and utilizing a quick-strike method with zero casualties, they had navigated through Mingyuan Residential Area, which had far more demons than the main street they had just passed. Surviving unscathed was truly remarkable.

"The density of demons outside the safety barrier will be even higher; everyone must stay alert," Mo Fan reminded them all.

Nine people moving together proved quite agile; unlike large formations where even turning a corner required significant effort.

"It looks like there are other mages fighting over there. Should we join them?" Zhang Xiaohou said from a high vantage point.

"They have their hands full and won't be able to pay us any mind. Let's keep moving forward," Mo Fan replied.

Just as Zhang Xiaohou was about to descend, he suddenly saw a massive wolf head slowly emerging from a flat building. The sharp canine bones sticking out from the head were so prominent!

Zhang Xiaohou's eyes widened, and his breath seemed to stop at that moment.

Heavens, it was a three-story flat house, but this wolf head was even higher! How enormous must this creature be? Was it just an ordinary demon??

"Zhang Xiaohou, what are you doing, come down quickly..." Wang Sanpang urged Zhang Xiaohou.

Zhang Xiaohou's entire body tensed up as he slowly moved behind the wall corner, giving everyone a "shh" gesture with eyes filled with sheer terror.

Just as Wang Sanpang was about to speak, Mo Fan swiftly covered this fat man's mouth.

Xue Museng seemed to realize something and quickly gestured for everyone to find cover.

The eight people hurriedly hid behind the wall corner, not daring to breathe, their faces a mix of shock and fear.

"Huh~~~~~~~~"

A puff of breath came out from not far away, instantly creating a chaotic whirlwind on the ground.

"Boom!!!"

"Boom!!!"

Heavy footsteps echoed, and with each step forward, everyone could feel the buildings shaking noticeably.

Zhang Xiaohou, hiding in the corner of the hallway, was frozen against the wall like a wooden puppet. His height was actually level with that massive wolf's head, and he even felt that the three-eyed monster sniffed towards him!

Damn it, if it had bitten down, it could have swallowed both this small room and everyone inside, okay?

Fortunately, the stench of garbage masked the smell of the people present, otherwise, given the creature's size, a swipe with its claw would have resulted in casualties.

"Boom!!!"

"Boom!!"

"Boom!"

His heart started pounding uncontrollably.

No one knew how far that creature had gone; everyone was still too shaken to move forward.

"Who…who the hell can tell me what that…that thing is!" Wang Sanpang gazed at the retreating head, his mouth trembling as he asked.

Zhang Xiaohou almost collapsed on the ground, his legs shaking uncontrollably.

It was too terrifying; how could there be such a monstrous creature in this world, which was far beyond the level of the giant-eyed squirrels and one-eyed demon wolves they had seen before? With that body type, their magic wouldn't even be able to injure others' flesh!

"It... it's a Warlord-level demon, the Three-Eyed Wolf, also known as the Bone Spike Rabid Wolf. Its three eyes almost have a 270-degree field of vision, and its steel-like sturdy body is covered in sharp bone spikes, making it an nightmare-level demon for us," Xue Museng said with a deep voice.

"This is something we can't possibly handle," Xu Zhao ting said with a hint of despair.

"Even mid-level mages would be killed by one hit if they aren't prepared in time. We must leave a message to inform the main force that there's a Warlord-level creature here, otherwise it will cause massive casualties," Xue Museng said.

Everyone nodded in agreement.

Mo Fan couldn't help but glance at the Silver Trade Building shrouded in mist after the rain.

If a Warlord-level Bone Spike Rabid Wolf was this terrifying, what about the Leader-level Sky Blue Wolves perched on top of the building's dome?

How powerful did mages need to be to withstand such demons?

...

...

The rain had stopped for a while, but mist still enveloped the city.

A kilometer south of the safety barrier, a female mage in a white uniform was running down the street, her body soaked with sweat. She was being fiercely pursued by two black-skinned monkey-faced monstrosities, as if they were about to tear this slender young woman into pieces at any moment.

"Wind Spiral! Cyclone!"

The air pressure suddenly dropped, and objects around started moving and spinning inexplicably!

The rotation was growing stronger, and soon a massive swirling air current formed into a wind dragon rising towards the sky. The car parked nearby slowly began to lift off the ground, streetlights were uprooted, and the two ugly monkey-faced demons chasing the woman screamed in agony before being mercilessly thrown into the air. The intense high-speed rotation made them sway like blades of grass.

“Boom~~ Boom~~~~ Boom~~~~~~~~~”

The car crashed heavily, metal fragments scattered everywhere. By the time those two monstrous creatures reached the air, they had already turned into a mess of bloody shreds, with crimson rain and ooze falling down, their deaths gruesome.

Seeing this scene, Lin Yuxin’s face broke into a weary smile, her gaze filled with some excitement as she stared at the male mage who had arrived in time.

"Mr. Yang, thank you!" Lin Yuxin said emotionally.

"If I’m not mistaken, those are the black beasts that the Black Church likes to summon," Yang Zuohé said gravely.

"That’s right; this disaster was their doing," Lin Yuxin stated confidently.

"We had guessed as much, but unfortunately, we had no defense and this Porcelain City… sigh." Yang Zuohé looked sorrowful. The blood-colored alarm had really been unexpected, and he didn’t know how many lives had been lost.

"I was attacked while guarding the Sacred Spring; their target seemed to be the Sacred Spring," Lin Yuxin said.

"We are aware of that; they specially sent me to meet you to ensure the safety of the Sacred Spring. So, what about the Sacred Spring? Did you give it to them?" Yang Zuohé asked.

Lin Yuxin shook her head and said, "They had surrounded me, but upon discovering that I didn’t have the Sacred Spring, they flew into a rage and tried to kill me. I escaped with the help of a pack of rampaging one-eyed wolf demons."

"You are quite smart, so what about the Sacred Spring?" Yang Zuohé continued to ask.

"I handed it over to the student cultivating at the Sacred Spring. I thought it would be safer on him than on me," Lin Yuxin said.

"Are you talking about Mo Fan, that naturally dual-talented brat?" Yang Zuohé asked in surprise.

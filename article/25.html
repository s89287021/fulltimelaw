It was Mu Ningxue!

Xu Zhaoting's face was filled with surprise and delight.

He hadn't expected that his exam had already caught Mu Ningxue's attention.

She must be quite surprised, after all, the power of the lightning element is unmatched by any other element.

Basic skills like releasing low-level lightning seals are just their foundation. "So, Xu Zhaoting still has a good chance of getting into the Imperial Capital Academy?" The principal, who was also skilled at climbing ladders, seized the opportunity to say.

The others immediately echoed him, lost in their own admiration.

"Unless something unexpected happens, Xu Zhaoting will likely be first in the school," said Chen Guangliang, the head of the student affairs office.

"That's not certain. As far as I know, Mu Bai from Class Eight is also a formidable opponent," said the vice-principal.

When the two said this, Mu He's face also broke into a smile. He immediately turned to Mu Zhuoyun and Mu Ningxue, saying, "Big brother, Mu Bai is our Mu family’s descendant; his performance has always been excellent. In my opinion, he should be close to finishing up there as well. Shall we go take a look?"

"It's the son of Mu Yang. Didn't Mu Yang pass away? I saw that mother and son were being bullied elsewhere, so I brought them to live in the manor. Mu Bai is also a very sensible and hardworking child; he’s not inferior to any other children in our family… oh, of course, there's a huge gap between him and Ningxue. However, he is still a good prospect for cultivation."

Mu He carefully explained to Mu Zhuoyun.

The manor was entirely under the control of Mu Zhuoyun. Although Mu He was considered a master, he was only a third or fourth in command and could not compare with Mu Zhuoyun.

"Oh, I have a vague memory of him. Then let's go take a look. What are the other board members' opinions?"

Mu Zhuoyun said.

The middle-aged sandy-haired chairman spoke up, "Since he is a descendant of the Mu family, we are indeed very interested."

A group of people began to move, slowly heading towards the exam hall of Class Eight.

These people had an overwhelming presence. To students, leaders might seem like distant figures, but to Mu Ningxue, who was a genius of their age, they were a wake-up call.

On the field, the boys immediately became energized when they saw girls watching, especially since this girl was the dream goddess of the entire school.

"Oh my God, the board members are coming over."

"They really came. My hands are shaking."

"It's perfect timing for me to show off."

"Show off what? Your assessment is already done, you scum who got a C!"

The students of Class Eight were no longer calm.

Everyone’s fate hung in the balance on this annual assessment, and just one approving glance from the board members could change their lives. As the board members approached, the exam atmosphere instantly became incredibly tense.

Mu He led the way at the front, and the bald examiner immediately looked up to meet Mu He's eyes.

Mu He gave Mu Bai a subtle hint, and the bald examiner immediately understood. Mu Bai pretended not to have seen anything and calmly said, "Next, Mu Bai!"

Mu Bai’s eyes were still fixed on Mu Ningxue. Even though they lived in the same castle, it was so large that Mu Bai had rarely stood this close to Mu Ningxue.

"Mu Bai, it's your turn," Mu He coughed to remind him.

Only then did Mu Bai realize what was happening.

There was no help for it; he had loved Mu Ningxue for too long. Even though he knew it was unlikely that they would end up together, he couldn’t tolerate being taken away by trash like Mo Fan. He had to be a protector!

It was time to prove all his hard work!

Mu Bai nodded and walked towards the examination room.

He had been waiting for this moment for a long time. Mu Bai took a deep breath.

The family’s recognition and Mu Ningxue’s gaze were what he had dreamed of all along, and both would come true today.

Mu Bai had indeed received extensive training in this area. Although his mind was extremely excited and tense at that moment, he still performed excellent meditation with his proficiency.

White ice rays rapidly spread out from the star-sensing stone and covered it entirely.

These ice rays were very intense, almost overflowing from the star-sensing stone and imprinting on Mu Bai’s face.

The entire class watched in awe.

Overflowing light was something they had never seen in so many exams. Even Zhou Min with her A++ grade paled in comparison to Mu Bai's overflowing light!

I will definitely make Chief and Mu Ningxue look at me in a new light!

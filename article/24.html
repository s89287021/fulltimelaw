"Teacher, I want to take another test," Zhang Xiaohou said firmly but somewhat shyly.

"Are you sure?" the examiner asked in surprise.

"I know that if my grade is high in the annual assessment, it will take longer to obtain a Stardust Artifact, so I need to take the release test," Zhang Xiaohou stated confidently.

Teacher Xue Musheng and Teacher Tang Yue still smiled, as they had long known that Zhang Xiaohou was already at the release level.

There were two tests in total.

The first one was the Stardust Test.

Most students can continue their studies at the Magic High School as long as they pass this stage. The second stage, however, is designed for top students; among the more than a thousand students, around a hundred have already reached the level of releasing skills in this field. If one can fully release their own school's skill during the examination, their annual assessment score will naturally be higher! To secure limited school resources, all students will put their best abilities on display during the annual assessment, leaving no secrets hidden. ... Zhang Xiaohou's release test was not particularly smooth. Despite his nervousness, he ultimately failed to connect the seven wind elemental stars into a star track. However, the examiners could see that this was a student who had mastered the release technique but faltered due to exam anxiety. Based on his performance, they still awarded him some additional points. Zhang Xiaohou walked out of the examination hall with a touch of disappointment, looking down slightly. Ms. Tang Yue patted his shoulder like a gentle big sister and said, "Don't be discouraged; with your scores, you're definitely going to get into the advanced class." "Teacher, I thought I was already very proficient. I didn't expect that even a little bit of nervousness would cause me to lose control of the stars each time, forcing me to start over.
